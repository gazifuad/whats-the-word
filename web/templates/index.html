What’s the Word? is a lecture-notes assistant web app that condenses hefty audio lectures and transcripts into manageable bullet-point summaries. Online education has generated millions of hours of Zoom lectures. Combing through these lectures for exam-relevant information is both boring and tedious. What’s the Word? offers a solution by summarizing hour-length videos into 1-page study guides, allowing students to review lecture material more conveniently, comprehensively, and efficiently.
